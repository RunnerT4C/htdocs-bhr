Community Partners & Sponsorship

Big Hill Runners is proud to partner with local businesses that support movement, community, and adventure. Our club is built around welcoming group runs, trail exploration, local events, and creating a place where all paces have a place.

We are always open to working with local businesses, clinics, coffee shops, breweries, running stores, and outdoor brands that align with our values. Sponsorship helps us create a stronger running community in Cochrane and beyond, while giving businesses a chance to connect with an active and engaged group of runners.

Why Partner With Big Hill Runners?

Big Hill Runners brings together people who care about health, community, trails, and supporting local. Through weekly runs, seasonal challenges, and social events, our club gives sponsors meaningful visibility in a genuine grassroots setting.

  • Exposure on our website and social media channels
  • Recognition at club events and community challenges
  • Direct connection to local runners and outdoor enthusiasts
  • Opportunity to offer member discounts, services, or product support
  • Association with a positive and growing local community
