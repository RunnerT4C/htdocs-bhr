Howdy! 👋

Big Hill Runners is a community-driven running club based in Cochrane, Alberta. We run the pavement, dirt, and everything in between — from town pathways to foothill trails. Whether you’re training for your first race, exploring new trails, or just looking for people to run with, you’re welcome here.

What we believe:

  • Running is better together
  • Hills make us stronger
  • All paces have a place
  • Consistency matters more than speed
  • Community comes first

What we do:

We organize regular road and trail runs throughout the week, with routes that highlight Cochrane’s pathways, foothills, and surrounding trails. Some runs are social and conversational, others are a bit more challenging — but the goal is always to get out and move together.

Who it's for:

Big Hill Runners is for runners who enjoy variety, value community, and don’t mind a hill or two along the way. If you like mixing road and trail, running year-round, and sharing miles with good people, you’ll fit right in. Our community includes runners of all experience levels, from first-time runners to those who have completed marathons, ultra distances, and 100-mile events.

Where we run:

We’re based in Cochrane, Alberta, at the edge of the foothills. Our runs reflect that — rolling terrain, open skies, river pathways, and trails that head west toward the mountains.

How to get involved:

The best way to get involved is to join us on social media, where we post run details, updates, and upcoming events. Come out for a run, introduce yourself, and see what we’re about.
