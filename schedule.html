🏃‍♂️ Wednesday Night Social Run

📅 Weekly • ⏰ 7:00–8:00 PM • 👟 ~10K Easy, Social Pace

Join Big Hill Runners every Wednesday evening for a relaxed mid-week group run in Cochrane, Alberta. This is an easy, conversational 10K — perfect for meeting new people, getting outside, and breaking up the week with some good company. New runners are always welcome — just show up, introduce yourself, and join in.

  • ⏰ When: Wednesdays, 7:00–8:00 PM
  • 📏 Distance: ~10 km
  • 💬 Pace: Easy / conversational (we regroup as needed)
  • 🛣️ Route: River Path to Mitford Wall and back
  • 👋 All paces welcome

We share the weekly meeting spot, route details, and any updates on our Facebook page. You can also reach out to info@bighillrunners.com if you have any questions.
